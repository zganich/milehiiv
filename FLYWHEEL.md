## Flywheel Mechanics

### 1. ACQUIRE — Driver Finds Us
**Channels:**
- Reddit posts (r/uberdrivers, r/doordash)
- Facebook gig groups
- TikTok "tax hack" videos
- Word of mouth from other drivers
- Google "uber mileage deduction"

### 2. ACTIVATE — Upload Data
**Friction killers:**
- No signup required for first analysis
- Accept CSV, screenshot, or manual entry
- Results in <30 seconds
- Mobile-friendly upload

### 3. AHA MOMENT — See Missing Money
**The magic:**
- "You have 8,400 untracked miles"
- "That's $5,628 in deductions you're missing"
- "At 22% tax bracket = $1,238 in your pocket"

**This is what makes them share.**

### 4. AMPLIFY — Share Result
**Built-in virality:**
- "Share your results" button
- Generates shareable image with their stats
- Pre-written tweet/post: "I was missing $X in mileage deductions 🤯"
- Referral program: "Give $10, Get $10"

### 5. LOOP — Friends Find Us
**The flywheel spins:**
- Friend sees share → visits site → uploads → sees $$ → shares
- Every user becomes a channel
