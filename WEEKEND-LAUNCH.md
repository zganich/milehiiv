## 💬 Comment Templates

**When someone asks "How do I track mileage?"**
> Great question! You can deduct 67¢/mile but most apps only track PAID miles (when you have a passenger). The drives to pickups and between trips are also deductible — that's usually 30-40% more miles. I use [tool] to find those gaps.

**When someone says "I forgot to track this year"**
> You're not alone — this is super common. Good news: if you have your Uber/Lyft trip history, you can reconstruct it. I built [tool] that imports your trip data and finds the gaps between rides. Those are deductible too.

**When someone asks "Is deadhead deductible?"**
> 100% yes. IRS allows you to deduct ALL business miles — commute to your first ride, between rides, and home from your last ride. Most drivers miss 30-40% of their deductions because apps don't track this.
